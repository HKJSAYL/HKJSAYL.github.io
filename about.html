Who are we?

HKJSAYL is an organization dedicated to fostering leadership skills among young people in Hong Kong. It unites students from various schools to promote collaboration, personal development, and active community involvement. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and leadership training, HKJSAYL empowers youth to become influential leaders, encouraging them to engage responsibly and creatively in their communities. The association aims to nurture a generation of socially conscious individuals who are equipped to drive positive change.

Our Aim

  • To provide a platform for youth engagement and leadership development through collaborative projects, skill-building workshops, and community service, empowering young leaders to make a meaningful impact in their schools and society.
