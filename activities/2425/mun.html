Eligibility

In order to be eligible to apply for HKYLMUN, applicants must be 13 years of age or older and enrolled in, or recently graduated from, a secondary school(or equiv) during the time of the conference. In particular, university/college students are not eligible to apply for HKYLMUN.

We understand that some high schools may culminate before the dates of the conference. You are eligible to apply to YLMUN as long as you have NOT received your University student ID during the time of the conference. (This requirement may be waived on a case by case basis)

Once accepted, school candidates(registration under school participant) must be accompanied by a faculty advisor unless exempted by the discretion of the Secertary-General. Teachers, university students, parents, administrators, and others aged 18 years or older are eligible to serve as faculty advisors. Any individual who is participating at HKYLMUN as a delegate is ineligible to serve as a faculty advisor.
